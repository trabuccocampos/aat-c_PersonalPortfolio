Title: The Pulse

----

Date: 2014-03-06

----

Intro: Tracking daily city life

----

Brief: Craft a new travel guide that can compete with the rest of the travel guide market.

----

Concept: 

*Can we track how a city lives day to day?*
Using the activity posted in social networks, The Pulse seeks to capture the life of the city and make sense of it. With the same information, The Pulse can also identify the up-and-coming places that are worth going to.
