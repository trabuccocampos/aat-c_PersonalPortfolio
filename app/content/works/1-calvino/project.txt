Title: Calvino

----

Date: 2014-07-01

----

Brief: Design a visual book using Italo Calvino’s “If On A Winter’s Night A Traveler”

----

Concept: 

Render Calvino’s text accessible, legible, and visually clear, yet interpreting his text.

Challenge typographic rules, book formats and layouts, and the use of photography in narrative volumes.
